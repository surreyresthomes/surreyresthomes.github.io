Surrey Rest Homes LTD

Surrey Rest Homes aims to create an atmosphere in which residents can live as normally as possible, to enhance their quality of life by providing a safe and comfortable environment, encouraging them in their interests and activities, to maintain a climate in which individuality, independence and personal dignity are respected. We provide warmth, good food and proper professional care at all times. Our overall aim is to ensure that all residents are happy and contented and to this end. We believe that there are certain basic values which contribute to quality of life for most people.

  • Privacy

    The right of every individual to be left alone undisturbed and free from intrusion or public attention into their affairs.

  • Dignity

    The right of every individual to be recognised and valued regardless of circumstances and to have their uniqueness and personal needs treated with respect.

  • Independence

    The right of every individual to act and think without reference to another person.

  • Choice

    The right of every individual to the entitlements associated with citizenship.

  • Fulfillment

    The right of every individual to realise personal aspirations and abilities in all aspects of daily life.

Private Bedrooms

Private Bedrooms for quiet and privacy

For perfect quiet comfort and privacy, all residents have their own tastefully decorated and furnished rooms. We are happy for residents to bring in their own items of furniture and other personal belonging such as ornaments and pictures to help create a genuine home from home atmosphere. Each room has a wash hand basin. Many rooms have en-suite facilities. All of the rooms have central heating radiators with a seperate thermostatic control, enabling the temperature to be adjusted to suit the individual’s requirements. In additition, each room is connected to a 24 hour emergency call system to summon help if required.

Home Cooking

All our meals are prepared on the premises. Various menus catered for including special diets.

Home Cooking



Breakfast is served in bedrooms or the dining room from 7.00am so residents can start the day in a leisurely fashion and at a pace to suit themselves.

Morning coffee is served either in the sittingrooms or if the weather is warm on the patio in the garden.

Lunch (preceded by a glass of sherry, for those who partake) is served in the dining room.

During the afternoon, residents can enjoy afternoon tea with home made cakes.

A light supper is served during the early evening and a comforting warm drink on retiring, rounds off the day.

Medical Care

Residents can be registered with local NHS or a private doctor of their choice. A chirodist visits regularly as does a hairdresser. These must be paid for by the residents at the time, or can be paid by us and charged at a cost, with a monthly account. We take every care that residents do not need to walk or travel far for day to day needs.
